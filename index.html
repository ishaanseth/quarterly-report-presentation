Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)

The formula used for our projections:

\[ \mathrm{CAGR} = \left( \frac{\text{Ending Value}}{\text{Beginning Value}} \right)^{\tfrac{1}{n}} - 1 \]
Clarify that \(n\) is years; tie to 5-year forecast.
