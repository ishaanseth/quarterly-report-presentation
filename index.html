Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)

The formula used for our projections:

\[ CAGR = \left( \frac{\text{Ending Value}}{\text{Beginning Value}} \right)^{\frac{1}{\text{n}}} - 1 \]
Explain that 'n' is the number of years. This formula is key to our 5-year forecast model.
